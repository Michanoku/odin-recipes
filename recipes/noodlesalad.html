Noodle Salad

Description

This noodle salad encapsulates the spirit of the rhineland in which it was born. Combine fresh ingredients with sausage, pickles and mayonaise. Your German grandma would be proud!

This recipe is a classic, beloved by many people. It has been often copied and remade, but the basic version remains the most popular to this day.

Ingredients

  • noodles (spirelli or any other shorter noodles)
  • sliced pickled cucumbers
  • sliced tomatoes
  • cubed bologna sausage
  • peas
  • mayonaise (salad mayonaise if you have it)
  • paprika powder
  • mustard
  • salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles until they are aldente
  2. Mix the mayonaise with paprika powder and mustard
  3. Add pickled cucumbers, tomatoes, bologna sausage and peas and mix well
  4. Add salt & pepper to your taste
  5. Add the noodles and mix everything well
  6. Cover and let rest in the fridge for a couple hours
  7. Enjoy!
