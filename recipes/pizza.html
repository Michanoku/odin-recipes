Fiddlehead Pizza

Description

This recipe takes the classic cheese pizza and adds a unique ingredient. Fiddlehead ferns. When going foraging for fresh vegetables, fiddlehead ferns are a favorite of many locals. Here we use them to create an interesting and fresh take on a vegetable pizza.

These ferns are best enjoyed after having picked them yourself during a nice hike, but you can also find them in local shops that sell foraged vegetables and mushrooms.

Ingredients

  • pizza dough (made or bought)
  • tomato sauce
  • fiddlehead ferns (cut off the stems if they are too long)
  • your favorite pizza cheese
  • oregano

Steps

  1. Lay out the dough to the shape of your liking
  2. Spread out the tomato sauce on the dough
  3. Place the fiddlehead ferns on your pizza
  4. Add the cheese
  5. Add the oregano
  6. Bake in the preheated oven at 200C for 14 minutes.
  7. Enjoy!
