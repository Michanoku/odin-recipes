Chirashi Sushi

Description

A fun take on sushi enjoyed with the whole family! You can serve this sushi in a huge tray for everyone to enjoy. Use nori sheets to wrap the sushi and eat it as finger food.

Your family will love this fun food at parties or gatherings. Easy to prepare and fun to eat, a hit with children and adults!

Ingredients

  • rice
  • rice vinegar
  • tuna
  • salmon
  • shrimp
  • crab
  • peas
  • cucumbers
  • carrot
  • nori seaweed

Steps

  1. Make the rice in your rice cooker
  2. Add the rice vinegar, carrots and peas and mix
  3. Place the mix in your tray
  4. Spread out the cucumbers, tuna, salmon, crab and shrimp over the rice
  5. Serve the with nori
  6. Enjoy!
